## Project Overview

- **Location**: Urban core, adjacent to main arterial road and secondary road
- **Site Area**: 9,450 m² (~14.2 mu)
- **Land Use**: Commercial / Business facility (Type B)
- **Total GFA**: 43,921.37 m²
- **Above Ground**: 37,807.32 m² (26 floors)
- **Underground**: 6,114.05 m² (1 floor)
- **Building Height**: 99.30 m

## Guestroom Breakdown

- **Standard rooms**: 150 (25 m² each)
- **Suites**: 30
- **Deluxe rooms**: 16
- **Budget rooms**: 11
- **Total**: 207 rooms

## Design Tensions

1. **Vertical acoustic conflict**: KTV/SPA (2F) and banquet hall (4F) below office (5F-15F) and guestrooms (16F-25F)
2. **Four-circulation separation**: Hotel guests, office workers, banquet visitors, service logistics must have independent access
3. **Core efficiency**: Single through-core must serve both office and hotel zones with different floorplate requirements
